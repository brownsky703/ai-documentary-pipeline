Create a 16:9 cinematic editorial animation illustration for an AI-assisted documentary scene.

Scene:
A massive Starship rocket lifts through dense clouds at sunrise while engineers and spectators watch from the ground. The image should feel informative, dramatic, and ready for a YouTube documentary about reusable rockets and the future of space infrastructure.

Style:

- Clean outlines
- Soft atmospheric lighting
- Full-bleed composition
- Documentary editorial mood

Production notes:
- Leave clean negative space for Korean subtitles near the lower third.
- Keep the rocket and launch plume readable at thumbnail size.
